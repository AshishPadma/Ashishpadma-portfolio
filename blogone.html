How to use google search engine like a pro

1. Quotation " "

Use quotes (" ") to search for exact phrases or words

Eg: "Amazon.com"

It is one of the most vital search tips, especially useful if you're trying to find results containing a specific phrase.

2. Asterisk *

Use * to fill missing words or phrases

Eg: "How to become * developer"

It is helpful if you are trying to determine a song from its lyrics, but you could not make out the entire phrase or if you are trying to find all forms of an expression.ss

3. Minus sign or hyphen

Use - to exclude results containing certain words

Eg: "Learn English - noun"

You'll want to eliminate results with certain words if you are trying to search for a term that is generating a lot of results that are not of interest to you. Figure out what terms you are not interested in and re-run the search

4. Site search

Using site: will search within a specific website

Eg: "site:Amazon.com red shirts"

Think of the site search function as a google search that searches only a particular website.

5. vs

Use vs to compare two things using this

Eg: "Rice vs Quinoa"

Cannot decide between Rice or Quinoa use this example and you will receive side by side comparisons of the nutritional facts.
